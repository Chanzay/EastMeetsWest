Thai House

Welcome to Thai House!

  • Thai House in Charlotte provides authentic Thai foods of all sorts. Their popular dishes included the very well known Pad thai, which is a type of noodle stir-fry. Each Thai house is designed to feel like you are actually in Thailand with many paintings and wooden art. There are currently three locations in Charlotte which are Pineville, uptown, and the university area. If you are in the area, Thai House is definitely a great place to stop by.
