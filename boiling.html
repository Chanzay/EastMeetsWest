Boiling Pot

Welcome to Boiling Pot!

  • Boiling Pot is a Chinese restaurant that serves a hot pot style of eating. Hot pot has traditionally been used in China culturally, typically as a family gathering. Here at hot pot you can order items that you would like with two soups of your choice. You will get to cook the food how you like with sauce provided as well. If you're looking for a way to celebrate with your family or friends come down to Boiling Pot in Charlotte!
